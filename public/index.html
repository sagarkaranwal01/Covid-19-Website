Daily guidelines

Four habits that still make the biggest difference

01 — Hygiene

Wash hands often

Use soap and water for at least 20 seconds, especially after being in public spaces.

02 — Distance

Keep your space

Maintain distance from anyone showing symptoms, and avoid crowded indoor settings.

03 — Masking

Mask in high-risk areas

Wear a well-fitted mask in healthcare settings, transit, and crowded venues.

04 — Vaccination

Stay up to date

Get boosters as recommended by local health authorities, especially if high-risk.

Symptoms to watch for

🌡️

Fever or chills

Often the first noticeable sign, sometimes paired with fatigue.

🫁

Cough or shortness of breath

Seek medical advice promptly if breathing becomes difficult.

👃

Loss of taste or smell

A distinctive early symptom reported across many cases.

💪

Body aches and fatigue

Rest and monitor closely; isolate if symptoms persist.
